── Asset Register & Lifecycle [3R · 1P] 4 caps
  1. 1) Asset Register & Hierarchy generic ✓ — Does your solution maintain a centralised asset register with hierarchical classification, unique identifiers, technical specifications, custodian tracking, and lifecycle metadata, so you have complete visibility for maintenance planning and compliance?
  2. 2) Asset Lifecycle Management generic ✓ — Does your solution track asset states through their full lifecycle, record key dates and transfers with audit trail, and retain complete historical records, so you maintain compliance and visibility across acquisition to retirement?
  3. 3) Depreciation & Valuation generic ✓ — Does your solution calculate depreciation using multiple methods, maintain separate book and tax schedules, automatically post entries to the general ledger, and track revaluations with audit trail, so you manage asset values accurately across reporting periods?
  4. 4) Asset Leasing 5/6 sectors — Does your solution calculate and maintain right-of-use assets and lease liabilities with re-measurement on term changes, payment schedules, and residual value tracking, for IFRS 16 compliance?
── Maintenance Operations (CMMS/EAM) [4R · 2P] 6 caps
  1. 1) Preventive Maintenance Planning generic ✓ — Does your solution generate scheduled work orders from preventive maintenance plans, support multiple maintenance strategies, and display upcoming activities with completion status, so you avoid unplanned equipment downtime?
  2. 2) Work Order Management generic ✓ — Does your solution create, assign, and close work orders for maintenance types, with spare parts reservation, labour tracking, failure codes, approval workflows, and contractor cost management, so technicians execute planned maintenance with full visibility and compliance?
  3. 3) Maintenance Requests generic ✓ — Does your solution enable non-maintenance users to submit service requests via multiple channels, categorise and prioritise requests at submission, track status with notifications, and attach supporting documentation, so service issues are captured and resolved without manual re-entry?
  4. 4) Spare Parts Management generic ✓ — Does your solution maintain a spare parts catalogue with multi-location tracking, automatic reorder alerts, work order reservations, and consumption history by asset, so you prevent stockouts and optimise maintenance spending?
  5. 5) Mobile Field Work generic ✓ — Can your solution enable technicians to execute work orders on mobile devices with offline capability, data capture functionality, and location stamping, so field teams remain productive without constant connectivity?
  6. 6) Offline Field Operations 3/6 sectors — Can your solution enable technicians to work offline with pre-downloaded work orders and assets, automatically resolve sync conflicts upon reconnection, and queue attachments for asynchronous upload, so field teams remain productive without connectivity?
── Compliance, HSE & OT Cybersecurity [1R] 1 cap
  1. 1) Inspection, Safety & Regulatory Compliance generic ✓ — Does your solution manage inspections and safety checks with configurable types and frequencies, record pass/fail outcomes, maintain audit trails, and generate expiry alerts, so you stay compliant with regulatory and insurance requirements?
── Reliability Engineering [1P] 1 cap
  1. 1) Condition Monitoring 5/6 sectors — Does your solution capture asset condition data from multiple sources, configure independent thresholds, automatically create maintenance work orders when limits are exceeded, and display historical trends with real-time alerts, so you shift from calendar-based to condition-based maintenance?
── Asset Analytics & Digital Twin [1R · 2P] 3 caps
  1. 1) Maintenance Analytics & KPIs generic ✓ — Does your solution calculate and report maintenance KPIs including asset and labour cost analysis, preventive schedule compliance, and downtime tracking by failure category, so you can monitor asset health and optimise maintenance planning?
  2. 2) Geospatial & GIS Integration 3/6 sectors — Does your solution display assets on an interactive map with layer control, synchronise coordinates from authoritative GIS sources, and enable work order creation and crew assignment based on asset location and geofence boundaries, for organisations managing distributed assets?
  3. 3) Spare Parts Optimisation 5/6 sectors — Can your solution analyse spare-parts inventory to recommend optimal stock levels and locations, calculate safety stock and economic order quantities, identify obsolescence risk, and model policy changes before deployment, so you balance stock-outs against carrying costs?
── Capital & Commercial Asset Management [4P] 4 caps
  1. 1) Warranty & Contracts generic ✓ — Can your solution track warranty and service contract terms, route in-warranty work orders to responsible vendors automatically, and link warranty claims to maintenance requests, so repair costs are correctly recovered and assigned?
  2. 2) Capital Projects & Commissioning generic ✓ — Does your solution track capital project costs and progressively capitalise assets as components enter service, with handover checklists and transfer of technical documentation to the asset register, so new assets transition to operational maintenance?
  3. 3) Asset SLA Management 5/6 sectors — Does your solution track contractual SLA terms per asset, calculate real-time uptime compliance excluding planned maintenance, monitor breach thresholds, and generate customer-facing compliance reports, so you can bill penalties or bonuses accurately?
